🏠 New Micropubs Opened in the UK (Late 2024 → Oct 2025)
1. From Beer To Eternity – Havant, Hampshire

Opened: Easter Weekend 2025
A music-themed micropub in the former Park Lodge, serving local Hampshire ales and craft beers. Family-run and decorated with musical memorabilia.
🔗 Portsmouth News Report

2. Stag & Scotch Micropub – Newport, Shropshire

Opened: 20 Dec 2024
Housed in a Grade II listed building on High Street. Currently trading while seeking retrospective planning approval. Focus on local real ales.
🔗 Shropshire Star Article

3. Holmewood Cricket Club Micropub – Derbyshire

Opened: 29 Mar 2025
Created to revive a village that had lost all pubs. Operated by Pete Grafton; serves Eyam Brewery ales. Limited hours (Thu–Sun).
🔗 Chesterfield CAMRA News

4. Original Gravity Micropub & Bottle Shop – Chesterfield

Opened: 2025 (date unspecified)
Run by Josh Clarke (Resting Devil Brewing). Offers 3 cask + 2 keg lines and 120 bottled beers; combines micropub & retail.
🔗 Chesterfield CAMRA News

5. Uckfield Signal Box Micropub – East Sussex (Planned)

Status: Planning permission granted Aug 2025
Three Acre Brewery to convert the old railway signal box into a micropub. Not yet open.
🔗 Nus Ghani MP Press Release

ℹ️ Context

Micropub Definition:
Small freehouse focusing on cask ales and conversation, without TV or music. First was The Butchers Arms, Herne (2005); now > 1,000 across UK.
🔗 Wikipedia Definition

Community Trend:
Recent openings revive disused sites and support local brewers (Holmewood Cricket Club, Havant’s Park Lodge rebuild, etc.).
